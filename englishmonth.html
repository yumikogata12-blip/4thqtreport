📖 English Month 🍵

Reflecting on English Month

English Month was an incredibly creative journey. Participating in the Door Book Cover activity allowed us to turn our classroom into a giant bookshelf, bringing our favorite stories to life through visual art. It was a wonderful exercise in teamwork and design.

The Booklandia event was equally magical. It felt like stepping into a different world where literature was celebrated in every corner. These activities not only improved our appreciation for language and storytelling but also strengthened the bond among my classmates. It was truly a memorable celebration of creativity and communication.
