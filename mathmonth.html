Reflecting on Math Month

Math Month was a challenge, but in the best way possible! From designing the Math Mural to showcasing our talent in Math Idol, we learned that numbers and logic are everywhere.

Creating the Math Garden and filming our Math TikTok fun facts really changed my perspective. It wasn't just about formulas on a board; it was about creativity and finding the beauty in mathematical patterns. I'm proud of how we made math fun and accessible for everyone!
