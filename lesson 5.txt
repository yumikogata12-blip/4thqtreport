<h2>Setting Dimensions</h2>
<p>This CSS will make both paragraphs and headings written in typeface Arial and colored green.</p>
<p>Can also be used to manage dimensions such as visibility, width or height of HTML elements.</p>

<h2>Property for Dimensions</h2>
<table>
<tr><th>PROPERTY</th><th>DEFINITION</th></tr>
<tr><td>visibility</td><td>If the element is visible or hidden</td></tr>
<tr><td>width</td><td>Width</td></tr>
<tr><td>height</td><td>Height</td></tr>
<tr><td>line-height</td><td>Line spacing</td></tr>
<tr><td>max-height</td><td>Maximum height</td></tr>
<tr><td>min-height</td><td>Minimum height</td></tr>
<tr><td>max-weight</td><td>Maximum weight</td></tr>
<tr><td>min-weight</td><td>Minimum weight</td></tr>
</table>

<h2>Pseudo-Classes</h2>
<p>Defined by a colon. They are not user-defined.</p>

<h2>Types of Pseudo-Classes</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Link</strong> - Hyperlinks that have not yet been visited</li>
<li><strong>Visited</strong> - Visited hyperlinks</li>
<li><strong>Hover</strong> - Hyperlinks when the mouse is hovered over them</li>
<li><strong>Active</strong> - Hyperlinks when they are clicked</li>
</ul>
